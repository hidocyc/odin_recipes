Pancake

Description

A pancake (or hotcake, griddlecake, or flapjack) is a flat cake, often thin and round, prepared from a starch-based batter that may contain eggs , milk and butter and cooked on a hot surface such as a griddle or frying pan, often frying with oil or butter.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Mix the dry Ingredients: combine flour, fugar, baking poweder.
  2. Mix the wet ingredients: Beat the eggs, melted butter.
  3. Make the pancakes: Add wet and dry ingredients. Add pancakes to pan and the serve.
