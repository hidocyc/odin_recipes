Fried Chicken

Description

Fried chicken, also known as Southern fried chicken, is a dish consisting of chicken pieces that have been coated with seasoned flour or batter and pan-fried, deep fried, pressure fried, or air fried.

Ingredients

  • 30 saltine crackers
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons dry potato flakes
  • 1 teaspoon seasoned salt
  • 1 egg
  • 6 skinless, boneless chicken reast halves
  • 2 cups vegetable oil for frying

Steps

  1. crush crackers. Add flour,potato flaes, salt and mix well
  2. Beat eggs. dregde chicken pieces in egg, then place in crumb mixture
  3. Heat oil
  4. Fry chicken, until golden brown
