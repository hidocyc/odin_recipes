Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Lasagna is a popular oven-baked pasta dish made of fresh egg pasta squares. It is a traditional dish from Bologna in Northern Italy, where the sheets are always green through the addition of spinach or nettles to the egg dough.

Ingredients

  • Meat: sweet italian suasage and lean ground beef
  • Onion and two cloves of garlic
  • Tomato products: can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato paste
  • Sugar
  • Spices:fresh parsely, dried basil leaves
  • lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
